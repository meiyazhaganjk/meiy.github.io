About Me

I am a Doctoral Researcher at, School of Mechanical and Materials Engineering, University College Dublin, Dublin, Ireland. I have experince in nonlinear dynamics and machine learning. My PhD project is on stochastic dynamics and chatter in metal cutting applications. The main research objective of my project is to develop a universal suite of numerical and analytical methodologies for the analysis and optimised performance of deterministic and stochastic non-smooth machining applications.
